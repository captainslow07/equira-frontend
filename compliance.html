Compliance

At Equira Labs, we are committed to operating our services in a responsible and compliant manner.

Responsible Data Practices: We process data only for its intended purpose and limit collection to what is necessary.

Data Protection: We implement reasonable safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Transparency: We aim to clearly communicate how our systems operate and how data is handled.

User Rights: Users may request access to or deletion of their data, subject to applicable requirements.

We design our systems to align with generally accepted standards and applicable regulations, and we continue to improve our practices as our services evolve.
